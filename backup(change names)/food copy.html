Food

Greece has many unique recipes that are unique to the land. It's good to be prepared in every way when traveling to another country. Here are some Greek cuisines to familiartize yourself with.

Spanakopita

These wonderful pastries are typically filled with feta cheese, fresh spinach, sweet onions and a bit of seasoning. They often come in different sizes, such as squares or triangles.

Souvlaki

Souvlakis are filling skewers of freshly grilled meat. Often made with beef, lamb chicken or fish. It tastes best with a side salad or bread.

Loukoumades

These little pastries are just like doughnut holes. They taste great with honey and almonds, and are best served fresh.
